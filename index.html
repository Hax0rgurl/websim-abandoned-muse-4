ABOUT

Welcome to Abandoned Muse

Abandoned Muse is a liminal and abandoned artist that has been exploring abandoned places since 2004.

I have a profound fascination with abandoned places and liminal spaces—those transient realms that exist on the threshold between the familiar and the unknown. Our journeys lead us through desolate buildings, overgrown structures, and silent corridors where time seems to stand still. By exploring these spaces, we seek to unveil the hidden narratives etched into their walls and the echoes of lives once lived.

Liminal places captivate us with their ambiguity and sense of transition. They are the in-between moments and locations that challenge our perceptions and inspire introspection. Through our art, we aim to capture the ephemeral beauty of these environments, inviting you to step into the void and embrace the unknown.
